Bootcamp 2021: City Simulator

My first programming project after the 2 weeks of bootcamp demonstrates the use and understanding of arrays, classes, loops and interactions with conditions. I have created a city scene, where I have used parameters to specify the posittion of each building and the use of loops to create many windows which can be turned on or off. I have used arrays to produce purple rain droplets as well as snow, which vary in both size and speed, to fabricate the impression of distance (the smaller the droplet/snow the slower they fall). I have also imbedded car images to produce a busy road and finally interactions to change the time of day.

Check out the code on Github: https://github.com/qrafiq1/citySimulator
